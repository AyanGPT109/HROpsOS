# HROpsOS: User Guide

HROpsOS is divided into three distinct portals. This guide explains how to use each portal based on your assigned role.

---

## 1. Worker Portal (Employee App)
**Target Audience:** Frontline workers, field operators, and general employees.

### Core Features
- **Dashboard:** View your current attendance status, worked hours, and schedule for the day.
- **Check-In / Check-Out:** 
  - To check in, click the **Check In** button.
  - The application will request your GPS location.
  - **Important:** You must be physically located within your assigned Plant/Site's geofence radius. Ensure you are outdoors or near a window so your device can achieve high GPS accuracy.
- **Leave Management:** Request casual, sick, or earned leave directly from the "Leave" menu. View the status of your pending requests.
- **Attendance History:** Review your past check-ins, check-outs, and total worked minutes for any given day.

---

## 2. Admin Portal (Company Administrator)
**Target Audience:** HR Managers and operations supervisors for a specific company/tenant.

### Core Features
- **Worker Management:** 
  - Navigate to the "Workers" tab to onboard new employees.
  - Assign employees to specific departments and plants.
- **Attendance Monitoring:**
  - View real-time dashboard statistics indicating who is currently present, absent, on leave, or late.
  - Filter history by worker or date to resolve discrepancies.
- **Leave Approvals:** Review incoming leave requests from workers. Approve or reject them with optional review notes.
- **Support Tickets:** Raise internal support tickets to the Super Admin if you encounter platform issues.

---

## 3. Super Admin Portal (Platform Owner)
**Target Audience:** Platform owners, system administrators, and technical support.

### Core Features
- **Tenant (Company) Management:** 
  - Onboard entirely new companies to the platform.
  - Deactivate or suspend companies based on subscription status.
- **Plant & Geofence Setup:**
  - Create geographical sites ("Plants") for companies.
  - Configure precise GPS Geofences. Click the Map Icon next to a plant to set the center coordinates, radius (e.g., 200 meters), and toggle the "Active" status.
- **Global Administrator Management:** Assign access rights to Company Administrators so they can manage their respective portals.
- **Platform Analytics & Audit:** View global usage statistics across all tenants and review immutable audit logs for critical actions.
